AnnaVlasova

Professional photographer.
I work in the genres of fashion, portrait, and art photography.

Currently based in St. Petersburg

I got interested in fashion, painting, and dance when I was a little kid. And my passion for photography has developed since then.

For as long as I can remember, I'm constantly trying to create something beautiful and interesting.

I started with painting, then I smoothly moved on to photography, and it took over my life.

I am still looking for inspiration in paintings, architecture, contemporary and classic art. But what inspires me most is people and everything about them, their individuality, feelings, complexity, the beauty of their bodies, and everything they create

The human is beautiful, both in static and in motion. This is what I'm trying to capture in my work.
